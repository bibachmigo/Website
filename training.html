Training & Placement

Training & Placement

The Institute has a training and placement cell headed by a placement officer who maintains regular connectivity with the industries/ organizations. In addition, our faculty members visit various organizations/ industries/ hospitals where our students undergo practical training, and interact with the concerned person in-charge at the organizations/ industries/ hospitals regarding possible job opportunities for our students.

During the industrial training period, many of our students get placement offers owing to their knowledge, hard work and sincerity during their training period in the organization. The institute keeps the students up-to-date about the different job fairs happening around NCR area. Also, seminars, workshops and industrial visits are conducted throughout the year to keep students up-to-date with the current job market. Mock interviews and group discussions are also being conducted at the Institute to help students with interview skills. Also, the Institute sponsors eligible students every year to participate in organized by the Department of Training and Technical Education, Govt. of NCT, Delhi.

With a large alumni network of AIT, current students have an advantage of getting information about more job opportunities.
