Introduction

Ambedkar Institute Of Technology

Ambedkar Institute of Technology popularly knowns as AIT. It was established by the Department of training and technical education (D.T.T.E.), Government of N.C.T and is also recognized by university grants commission(UGC). It offers a diploma in four different specializations whereas BCA and B.Voc are the new initiatives of the institute. The institute's total enrollment capacity is 1304. Admission to these courses is entrance based. Applicants seeking admission to the institute may apply online. The institute is well equipped with all the facilities with the motive of providing premier experience and learning to their students.
